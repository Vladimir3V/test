Frequently asked questions

What is Lykke Wallet?

It's like your normal wallet or purse, only it's electronic. You use it to hold your money in different currencies. It's very practical: You can exchange one currency to another, and you don't need to go to a bank or an exchange to do it. Your mobile wallet cryptocurrency app is linked to the Lykke Exchange. You can buy shares with it and even issue your own.

Why would I need it?

You need Lykke Wallet if:

  • You want to invest in digital assets.
  • You want to exchange currencies and cryptocurrencies.
  • You want to profit from trading.
  • You want to profit from downturns in the market through margin trading.

What do I need to pay for?

Nothing. There is no charge to use Lykke Wallet, and the commission rate is zero percent.

What if I am no good with technology?

You don’t need any special knowledge. If you use the Internet, social media, online banking, or other apps on your smartphone, you are skilled enough to manage your Lykke Wallet.

Is it safe to put my money into Lykke Wallet?

Unlike traditional exchanges, you don’t need to trust your bitcoins to the marketplace. Lykke Wallet is a multisignature wallet, which is like a safe with two keys. You have one key, and the exchange has the other. No one can take your money, even if your personal key is stolen.

What if anything happens to the exchange?

When you put your money into a Lykke Wallet, Lykke emails you a signed refund transaction. If the exchange is compromised, you’ll get your money back within two weeks.

How can I get my money out of Lykke Wallet?

You can withdraw your money to your account at any time without paying a fee.
